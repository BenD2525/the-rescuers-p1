A shelter specialised in matching dogs to humans

Rossi

Rossi is an old boy, but very loving. He was born in Greece and spent the first year of his life there as a hunting dog before coming over to the UK. He has been well looked after but now is the time for him to find a new home. He is old and so does not require too much exercise, and likes his peace and quiet. If you would like a quiet, funny, loyal friend then Rossi is for you.

Lucy

Lucy is a young lady who had a difficult start in life. She was found abandoned in a park when she was a puppy and has been with us ever since. She is very energetic and loves to play, she would be well suited to a family with young children.

Bob

Bob is a ball of energy. He was given to us when his owner died in 2020 and his family could no longer care for him. He loves to run around and socialise with other dogs. He can be fearful of humans so will need some patience, however he is a loving boy who would make a great companion.

Charlie

Charlie is our oldest dog, and suffers with arthritis having had a tough life before we took care of him. He was used in dog fights and has been traumatised as a result. Understandably, he is very wary of new people and would need to be with an older family with a lot of patience.
