A shelter specialised in matching dogs to humans

History

In 2020, due to the ongoing effects of the pandemic we became aware of a large number of dogs and other animals which could no longer be taken care of due to the decline in physical and mental health of their owners.

Mission

  • To provide a caring home for as many stray dogs as we can
  • To help create long-lasting friendships between dogs and people
  • To educate people on the benefits that caring for a dog can bring

Values

  • Happiness
  • Compassion
  • Animal Rights
