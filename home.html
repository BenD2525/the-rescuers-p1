A shelter specialised in matching dogs to humans

Dogs in need

We are a small, family-run dog-rehoming service based in London whose focus is on caring for unwanted dogs until they can find their forever home. When so many dogs need homes, why buy a puppy? Come and see some of our dogs that need loving homes.

Health

Caring for a dog has shown to have numerous positive benefits for both mental and physical health. Studies have shown that the exercise associated with walking a dog lowers blood pressure, while having a four-legged friend can greatly improve your mental health.
